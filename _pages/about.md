I am a pulmonary physician and physician-scientist based in Japan. I practice
respiratory medicine at **Kobe City Medical Center General Hospital**, and I am a
PhD candidate at the **Graduate School of Medicine, Nara Medical University**.

My work sits at the interface of the clinic and the laboratory. Clinically, I care
for patients with **interstitial lung disease (ILD)** and **idiopathic pulmonary
fibrosis (IPF)**. In the laboratory, I study the **fibroblast biology** that drives
lung fibrosis — including the roles of **Angiopoietin-like 4 (ANGPTL4)** and
**Insulin-like growth factor 1 (IGF-1)** in disease development.

I am especially interested in *why fibrosis behaves as a shared program across
organs*, and in building a research perspective that connects mechanism, prognosis,
and the patient's experience. You can read more on my [research vision](/research/).

## Research interests

- Idiopathic pulmonary fibrosis (IPF)
- Interstitial lung disease / idiopathic interstitial pneumonia
- Lung fibrosis and fibroblast biology (ANGPTL4, IGF-1)
- Cross-organ fibrosis
